01 — Editor's Note

About the strategist behind the work.

Hi, I'm Sophia, I've always been fascinated by what makes people stop scrolling, lean in, or decide something is worth their time. Marketing became my way of studying that fascination in public.

I work at the intersection of storytelling and strategy. I build positioning, shape launches, and create campaigns designed to earn attention rather than demand it. The goal isn't simply to be seen but also to be remembered.

Most of my work revolves around a simple question: why do people care? The answers usually live somewhere between psychology, culture, and a well-timed message.
