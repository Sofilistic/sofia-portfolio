01 — Editor's Note

About the strategist behind the bylines.

I grew up between two cities and one obsession: noticing what made people stop scrolling, leaning in, or actually walking into a cinema on a Tuesday. My career has been a long-running attempt to industrialize that noticing.

I lead brand and growth at the intersection of taste and arithmetic. I write briefs that read like short stories, ship campaigns that respect the audience's intelligence, and run experiments that tell me something true — not just something flattering.

I've launched three consumer brands from zero, marketed independent films into wide releases, and rebuilt a Series B's growth engine without buying another impression. I'm most useful when the problem is unclear and the stakes are high.
